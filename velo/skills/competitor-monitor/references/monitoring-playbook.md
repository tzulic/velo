# Competitor Monitoring Playbook

## SaaS Competitors

**Weekly checks:**
- Pricing page: `"[name] pricing"` — look for tier changes, new plans, price increases
- Changelog: `"[name] changelog OR release notes OR what's new"` — new features
- Blog: `"[name] blog"` — thought leadership, case studies, announcements
- Careers: `"[name] careers OR jobs"` — hiring velocity, new departments

**Monthly checks:**
- G2/Capterra reviews: `"[name] site:g2.com OR site:capterra.com"` — sentiment shifts
- Tech stack: `"[name] site:builtwith.com"` — technology changes
- Funding/news: `"[name] funding OR acquisition OR partnership"` — strategic moves

## E-Commerce Competitors

**Weekly checks:**
- Product catalog: `"[name] new arrivals OR new products"` — product expansion
- Promotions: `"[name] sale OR discount OR coupon"` — pricing strategy
- Shipping: `"[name] shipping policy OR free shipping"` — logistics changes

**Monthly checks:**
- Reviews: `"[name] reviews"` — customer sentiment
- Marketplace presence: check Amazon, Shopify app store listings

## Agency Competitors

**Monthly checks:**
- Case studies: `"[name] case study OR client results"` — wins, methodology
- Team page: `"[name] team OR about us"` — growth, new hires
- Services: `"[name] services"` — new offerings, positioning changes
- Client logos: check homepage for new client logos

## How to Compare

When reporting findings, structure as:
1. **What changed** — factual description
2. **Why it matters** — impact on competitive positioning
3. **Recommended action** — what the user should consider doing
